Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are your technicians licensed and insured?

We ensure that our team is highly trained, experienced, and complies with all local regulations to guarantee safe and reliable service. Their experience level will appear to you before requesting the service.

2. How do I schedule a service?

You can easily schedule a service by calling the technician directly when you press on the sub-service needed. Once the technician answer, he/she will contact you to confirm the details, location and time for the repair.

3. Do you provide emergency repair services?

Yes! We offer 24/7 emergency repair services for issues such as plumbing leaks, electrical malfunctions, and more. If you're facing an urgent problem, don't hesitate to contact us, and we'll send a technician to your location as quickly as possible.

4. What types of home repair services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of home repair services including plumbing, electrical repairs, carpentry, painting, drywall installation, flooring, and general maintenance. Whether it's an emergency or a planned project, our team is ready to assist with various home repair needs.

5. How much will my repair cost?

The cost of your repair depends on the scope and nature of the service required. We provide free estimates for most services, and we'll discuss the costs with you upfront before any work begins. We aim to offer competitive pricing while ensuring high-quality repairs.
